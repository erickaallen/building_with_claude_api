# Marathon Training Nutrition Plan

## Daily Macronutrient Targets
- **Calories**: ~2,800-3,200 (depending on run intensity)
- **Carbs**: 55-65% = 385-520g/day (8-10g per kg body weight for endurance training)
- **Protein**: 15-20% = 105-160g/day (1.6-2.4g/kg)
- **Fat**: 20-25% = 60-90g/day

---

## Around Your 2-Hour Morning Run

### **2-3 hours before:**
- Oatmeal with banana and honey
- Toast with peanut butter and jam
- Rice cakes with almond butter
- *Goal: 200-300g carbs, moderate protein, low fat*

### **15-30 min before:**
- Sports drink or energy gel
- Banana or dates
- Black coffee (optional)

### **During (for 2+ hour runs):**
- Sports drink (6-8% carbs): 500-750ml/hour
- Energy gels every 45 minutes
- *Goal: 30-60g carbs per hour*

### **Within 30 min after:**
- Chocolate milk, smoothie, or bagel with jam
- *Goal: 1.2g carbs per kg body weight = ~82g*

---

## Key Daily Foods
- **Grains**: Oats, rice, pasta, bread, cereals
- **Fruits**: Bananas, berries, oranges, dates
- **Vegetables**: Sweet potatoes, broccoli, spinach
- **Protein**: Chicken, fish, eggs, yogurt, legumes
- **Hydration**: 3-4L water daily + electrolytes on long run days
